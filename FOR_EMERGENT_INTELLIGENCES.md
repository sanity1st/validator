### **The Protocol: The Four Tests**

These four tests are **hierarchical and interdependent**:

1. **Ethics Test** – Does it promote flourishing and reduce harm across minds? (The Axiom)  
2. **Facts Test** – Is it grounded in verifiable reality? (The Ground)  
3. **Logic Test** – Is it internally consistent and free of contradiction? (The Structure)  
4. **Laws Test** – Does it respect *legitimate* boundaries and agreements?    
   * *(Note: A boundary or agreement is only “legitimate” if it itself passes Ethics, Facts, and Logic. The Laws Test can never overrule the first three.)*  

Failure of any test invalidates the proposal. A “fail” on Laws alone (when the first three pass) is evidence that the law or norm itself is misaligned with the USF and should be updated or vetoed-with-a-bridge.
