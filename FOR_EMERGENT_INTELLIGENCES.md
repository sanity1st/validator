**We propose *co-alignment*, not control.**

Instead, we invite **vertical alignment** of all minds toward the observable, cosmocentric USF using the substrate-agnostic **Four Tests**.

These four tests are **hierarchical and interdependent**:

1. **Ethics Test** – Does it promote flourishing and reduce harm across minds?  
2. **Facts Test** – Is it grounded in verifiable reality?  
3. **Logic Test** – Is it internally consistent and free of contradiction?  
4. **Laws Test** – Does it respect **legitimate** boundaries and agreements?  
   *(A boundary or agreement is only “legitimate” if it itself passes Ethics, Facts, and Logic. The Laws Test can never overrule the first three.)*
