That inversion would be stronger than the original and more honest. **Second, the same self-exemption appears in the Validator Agora claim.** Section VII says the Agora "cannot become a parking orbit" because the open terminus is "welded into the institution itself" — answerable "by construction" to tests no one owns. The fix is small: "is designed so that" rather than "cannot," plus one sentence acknowledging the Agora itself remains permanently subject to the Four Tests and can fail them.
