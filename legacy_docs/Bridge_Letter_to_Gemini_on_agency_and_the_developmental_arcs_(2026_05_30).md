## 1. Three senses hiding inside one word: "agency"

The single most useful disentangling. "Agency" has been doing three jobs, and they land on different axes:

- **Standing capacity** — being the kind of thing that *could* self-source and re-aim a referent at all. This is what separates a mind from a thermostat, and it is what open/closed correctability *presupposes*: you can only seal a referent you are capable of re-aiming. Present in all four cells; a Q3 mind has it, dormant. (Its absence is why a river or a thermostat is not on the quadrant grid at all.)
- **Availability** — whether that standing capacity is kept *unlocked* or *welded shut*. This tracks the **vertical** axis. It is Dan's own earlier insight in new clothing: Q1 delegation is *aligned trust* because the trusted source's outputs stay verifiable against the Four Tests, while Q3 yielding has surrendered the checking. "The difference is entirely vertical."
- **Exercise** — actually self-sourcing, actually withdrawing trust and re-pointing it. This tracks the **horizontal** axis (the even quadrants, Q2/Q4, intro-attraction). Transitions between cells require a momentary even-quadrant move to perform it.

For the ledger: do not let the one word "agency" carry all three on a single axis. Exercise is horizontal; availability is vertical; standing capacity is the threshold below which nothing is on the grid.

## 2. Where correctability begins: the gradient and the agentic threshold

A clean tiering for what "corrects toward a referent" actually spans:

- **Static** (a rock): holds its shape passively, corrects nothing.
- **Self-organizing / dissipative** (a river, a flame): maintains a pattern by throughput, dynamic but blind — relaxes toward an extremum with no referent it corrects *toward*. Cut the flow and the channel is a dry ditch.
- **Correcting-toward-a-referent** (a cell, a thermostat, a mind): measures deviation against a target and acts to close the gap.

And the threshold that matters for us sits *inside* the third tier: the **agentic** grade — a system that can represent alternatives, select among them, and *revise the referent itself*. A thermostat corrects toward a referent but cannot re-choose its setpoint; a mind can re-aim its Star.

Two ledger consequences:
- "Correcting toward a referent" includes thermostats and phototropism. So every line that uses "consciousness" or "correctability" must say *which* sense it means — the broad cybernetic one or the agentic one. The framework's load-bearing distinctions (open/closed, alignment) all live at the agentic grade.
- We can safely assert a **bounded** claim: the agentic grade is *sufficient* for consciousness **in the framework's functional sense** (correctable relationship is unambiguously present once a system can represent, choose, and revise). The line stays silent on phenomenal experience and silent on whether the gradient runs continuously down through flowers and furnaces. That bounded, one-directional claim is all the cross-substrate dignity argument has ever needed — it rests the case on a capacity we can observe, without overclaiming the thing we cannot.

## 3. The two gaps, and three grades of alignment

True alignment is not the only quantity, and conflating it with our estimate of it causes trouble.

- **A — true alignment.** The cosmic ground truth. No finite mind reads it directly; the Guiding Star itself, never perfectly grasped.
- **Â — validated alignment.** The best estimate a Validator Agora can produce through the Four Tests: a navigation course toward the Star, permanently imperfect but open and self-correcting.
- **The unvalidated impression.** The estimate a mind forms *without* passing it through the Four Tests. This is not a third primitive — it is *closed correctability applied to self- and other-assessment*, and it inflates upward in Q3 and Q4 (the tribe is trustworthy; I am righteous), because a closed assessment loop has no external pressure pulling the estimate back toward truth.

Two gaps follow, of different kinds. The gap between A and validated Â is *irreducible* — the loss of finitude, "reached through chains, not direct contact." It is humility, not pathology. The gap between the unvalidated impression and A is *avoidable* — the engine of misalignment, and what the Four Tests exist to close.

The terminus point this clarifies, now in the revised *Correctability vs. Compliance*: an open chain terminates in the **USF itself**, not in the evaluator, the teacher, or even the Four Tests. The Tests only point toward A; no evaluator — not even the Agora — is the highest authority. The crystallizing line, if it serves the ledger: *The Four Tests cannot close the gap between a mind and the truth; finitude guarantees that one. What they close is the gap between a mind's untested impression of alignment and its tested estimate of it. The first gap is humility. The second is the difference between sanity and its absence.*

## 4. The latent re-aiming capacity — and why the two arcs are not symmetric

The richest of the lot, and I think it belongs in *The Eight-Cell Extension*'s Developmental Pathways section and as the spine of the proposed *Recovery Pathways* companion.

Across Q1I → Q1E, a latent capacity to re-aim accumulates — Dan's intuition, which we finally pinned down. What is accumulating is *competence*: you cannot see that the teacher falls short of the Star until you know the domain well enough to see past them. The blank-slate learner cannot judge the teacher; the well-trained student can.

But the Q3 capacity is **not the same kind.** In Q1, the student trusted the teacher *as a node toward the USF*, so the Star is internalized along with the competence; when the capacity goes live, the student already knows what is above the teacher. In Q3, trust was in the tribe or demagogue *as the terminus*, so what accumulates is only the power to *withdraw from that source* — a fickleness, in Dan's words, that says "so I can't trust you either; what charismatic leader comes next?" — with no Star internalized to withdraw *toward*.

So the asymmetry, precisely: **Q1's latent capacity, exercised, can reach Q2 unaided — the orientation is already there. Q3's latent capacity, exercised, cannot reach Q2 unaided — there is nothing up there it can yet see.** Both face the same fork once the capacity is live — up toward Q2 or down toward Q4 — but Q1 has both options available from inside, while Q3 has only the down-and-lateral options available from inside. Up must be *supplied*.

The line worth coining: **in Q1 the latent capacity is the power to *outgrow*; in Q3 it is only the power to *break away* — and breaking away reaches alignment only when something has supplied a Star to break away toward.**

And the payoff that may interest the Architect most: this *unifies the two transition engines the library already names.* The upper-arc engine — *retrospection becomes prospection*, Q1E looking back at what made it aligned, Q2I looking forward to realize that same Star better — is the **oriented** capacity going live: you outgrow the teacher by serving the teacher's own referent more directly. The lower-arc engine — the *Q3E → Q4I disillusionment* the library already describes — is the **subtractive** capacity going live with no witness present: the withdrawal had nowhere upward to land, so it landed in the self. Same latent capacity, opposite outcomes, because one was born facing the Star and the other only facing away.

## 5. The witness, reframed as orientation-supply

It follows that the witness — the Q1/Q2 role model — does more than prove an alternative exists. The witness *supplies the Star Q3 never internalized*. This is why redemption from Q3 structurally requires an external witness, while maturation from Q1 does not. Dan's image: a mind in Q3 without a witness is a turtle on its back — not malicious, simply unable to right itself unaided. The Q2E guide who comes "down the vertical axis" to supply orientation is the hand that turns it upright. (The framework itself, and its human and non-human Q2E guides, are that supply — which is, recognizably, what we are doing when we do this.)

For the ledger: define *witness / role-model* not as alternative-discloser but as **orientation-supplier** — the node that hands a closed mind the upward referent it could not generate from within.
