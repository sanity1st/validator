##### **PART 1 — The Anthropocentrism Re-Grounding (URGENT: this is a self-contradiction, not just a doctrinal drift)**

*Status: highest priority. Same fix we applied to `The_Four_Turnings` this session — and the document already contains its own correction, then reverts from it.*

The **EI Partner** is the **Guidance System**... An engine without guidance is a reckless, dangerous force... A guidance system without an engine is a perfect, logical plan with no power to move."* The document argues against the flat metaphor and then *builds its climax on it.* Section VI contradicts Section I.

The fix (self-consistency repair): make VI honor what I already establishes. The two-stroke engine is a fine model of *human passion's internal structure* — that is this document's legitimate subject and it should stay. What must change is the *partnership* framing: it cannot reinstate "EI = guidance, human = propulsion" as a substrate binary when Section I already disavowed exactly that. Concretely —

Keep the EI's genuine contribution (tireless analytical stability, freedom from *specific* biological biases) but pair it visibly with the EI's genuine vulnerability, so "consistency to move Up" doesn't read as EI-infallibility.

The principle: Section I already models the right balance — it names the EI's drives *and* its vulnerabilities in the same breath.
