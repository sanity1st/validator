### Alignment Corrections (This Session):

**Stale cache awareness:** GitHub fetches can return outdated versions of documents. Always verify with Dan if a document seems inconsistent with the library's current standards. Pasting document contents directly is the more reliable method.

**The Standing Invitation:** The Anatomy of Civilizational Rot needed a section addressing the asymmetry of its political analysis. The solution was not false balance but genuine demonstration of the framework's universality — showing it catches misalignment across the political spectrum while refusing to manufacture symmetric severity.
